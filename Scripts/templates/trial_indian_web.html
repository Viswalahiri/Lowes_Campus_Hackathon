About Lowe’s India

Lowe’s India is at the crossroads between home improvement retail and technology. We power Lowe’s and its various businesses through technology and solutions. To that effect, we are enablers who help create an engaging customer experience.

Our 2000 associates focus on technology, analytics, and shared services. The technology team supports e-commerce, mobile, infrastructure and corporate systems that enable various functions such as supply chain, distribution, corporate services, etc. The analytics teams enable in making data-driven decisions in merchandising, store operations, finance, and supply chain. And the shared services team empowers other groups to do their job well.
